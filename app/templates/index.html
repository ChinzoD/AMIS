AMIS

Welcome to AMIS, a freely available image database of gene expression in the mouse. AMIS contains over 1000 high-resolution images of fluorescently labeled orphan GPCR genes.

AMIS is provided by the Roth Lab at UNC Chapel Hill and the Shoichet/Irwin labs at the University of California San Francisco (UCSF).
